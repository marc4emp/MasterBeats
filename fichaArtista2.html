CASSIMM


I’m a musician, I spent my youth learning every instrument I could find along the way. I was also a member of a band for a few years playing the bass guitar. At 16 a friend of mine played me a tape of a Roger Sanchez DJ set and I literally fell in love with it. I didn’t have a clue or think it was possible that one man could “play” all of those instruments alone until I discovered what DJ’s were really doing behind the decks. I’ve made a lot of tracks recently, some of them I have already signed with labels like Dirtybird, Glasgow Underground and Lapsus Music. It’s hard when you make a lot of tracks, you would like to sign them straight away but on the other side, having too many releases close to each other could be a negative point so better slow down a bit, as they say “ good music never gets old”. CASSIMM’S ‘Sunday’ Ep is out now on Mother Recordings.
